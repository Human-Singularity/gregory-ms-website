GregoryAi continuously finds, cleans, and organizes peer-reviewed papers and clinical trials so you can move from searching to understanding, faster.

Partner with us
Gregory-MS does not provide medical advice. Always talk to your clinician before making health decisions.

Our story

In 2019, our founder Bruno Amaral was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis. Like many people with MS, he faced a flood of information and a shortage of clarity. In 2021, Gregory-MS.com was launched as a focused, transparent way to track what truly matters: rigorous studies, promising results, and clinical trials. We build it openly so others can learn from — and build on — our work.

Gregory-MS is stewarded by a small, volunteer-driven team and supported by an open-source codebase. The Lisbon Collective is Bruno’s consultancy and helps cover running costs so the project can keep serving the MS community.

What GregoryAi does

  • Continuously collects academic articles and clinical trials related to Multiple Sclerosis.
  • De-duplicates, standardizes titles/abstracts, and tags content by topic for easier discovery.
  • Surfaces potentially relevant items using machine-learning models and simple heuristics.
  • Lets you follow subjects and receive curated newsletter updates.
  • Stays transparent about where our data comes from and how we rank it.

For clinicians and researchers

  • Track new papers and trial registrations without the noise.
  • Follow fellow researchers and clinicians via RSS feed.
  • Export or integrate results into your own tools and workflows.

For people with MS and caregivers

  • Receive research updates by email.
  • Follow topics you care about (e.g., remyelination, symptom management).
  • Bring better questions to your next clinical appointment.

Sources

See the full, always-up-to-date list of sources we track — journals, registries, and feeds — on our dedicated page: gregory-ms.com/sources/.

How we work and how we’re funded

  • Open-source: our code is public and reusable — GitHub repository.
  • Privacy-first: we don’t sell data or ads; we publish what we index.
  • Community-funded: donations keep servers, email, and maintenance running. Read our transparency notes at gregory-ms.com/donations/ or donate via Stripe.
  • Transparent: we publish annual activity reports to keep you informed.
